About Me

If you asked my mom what humans are meant to do it is to "serve the highest good of others." I want my work to do just that.

I started to create music mainly because I didn't just want to perform it. I have always had a passion for writing. I would typically write songs and tell stories that would help me get through my day or struggles and I thought someone out there needs it too.

I typically chose projects that I know would have a positive impact. Whether it be a gospel song, or making a funny video, I want to make projects that will leave a good impact on the world. I try to use my platform as a way to share my faith in Jesus, spread love, and bring joy in a dark world.
