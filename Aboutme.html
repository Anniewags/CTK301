About Me

If you asked my mom what humans are meant to do it is to "serve the highest good of others." I want my work to do just that.

I started to create music mainly because I didn't just want to perform it. I have always had a passion for writing. I would typically write songs and tell stories that would help me get through my day or struggles and I thought someone out there needs it too.

I typically chose projects that I know would have a positive imact. Whether it is releasing a song that deals with heavy topics, or mainly just cause I know it would bring a laugh. I try to use my platform as a way to share my faith, spread love, and bring joy in a dark world.

I know whenever I wrtie a song I know it will have a positive impact on a person. Especally songs I write for me. I always would realse a song because I know that it can help change someone's life for the better.
