Performing

I’m a classically trained Soprano for many years. Previously I studied under Emily Elsworth in Anima GECC and Lee Kesslemen in the College of DuPage chamber singers. I'm also an Equity member and have been since 2013. I have been acting for many years as well and got training from Joe Barrasso from MBM talent and Roberta James from Wheaton Warrnville South high school. Currently I sing in Illinois State University’s Belle Voix. I also sing with Waymen AME church and DuPage AME church. My most recent role was in Illinois State’s production of Your a Good Man Charlie Brown as a swing for Schroeder, Lucy and Charlie Brown.
